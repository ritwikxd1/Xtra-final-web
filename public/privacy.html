Privacy Policy for Xtra Bot

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data Collection

Xtra bot does not store, collect, or share any personal user data. We do not log or save message content. Messages are only read and processed in real-time strictly for Automod, Auto-Responder, and Leveling features, and are immediately discarded after processing.

2. Data Usage

Any data processed by the bot (such as User IDs for leveling or warnings) is used solely for the core functionality of the bot to manage the server effectively and provide the promised utilities.

3. Data Retention

We do not retain any message content off-platform. Server administrators have the choice to disable automod, auto-responder, or leveling modules at any time if they do not wish for the bot to process real-time messages.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data or this privacy policy, please reach out via our official Discord Support Server.
